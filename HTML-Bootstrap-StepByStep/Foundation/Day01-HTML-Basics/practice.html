Exercise 5: Recipe Structure

Task: Structure a recipe using proper HTML elements.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

A classic homemade cookie recipe that's perfect for beginners.

Ingredients:

Dry Ingredients:

• 2 cups all-purpose flour

• 1 tsp baking soda

• 1 tsp salt

Wet Ingredients:

• 1 cup butter, softened

• 3/4 cup brown sugar

• 1/4 cup white sugar

• 2 large eggs

• 2 tsp vanilla extract

Add-ins:

• 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions:

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).

Step 2: Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.

Step 3: Cream butter and sugars until fluffy.

Step 4: Add eggs and vanilla to butter mixture.

Step 5: Combine wet and dry ingredients.

Step 6: Fold in chocolate chips.

Step 7: Drop spoonfuls on baking sheet.

Step 8: Bake for 9-11 minutes until golden brown.

Cooking time: 10 minutes | Prep time: 15 minutes | Serves: 24 cookies
