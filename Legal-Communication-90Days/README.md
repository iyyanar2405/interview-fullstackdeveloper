# 90-Day Legal Communication Fluency (Step-by-Step)

Practical, daily plan to strengthen writing and communication for lawyers, in-house counsel, and legal ops.

## Program goals (outcomes by Day 90)
- Plain-English drafting without losing legal precision
- Professional client and stakeholder communication (email, memos, status)
- High-quality legal documents (memos, contracts, notices, responses)
- Effective negotiation and litigation communications
- Cross-cultural tone and media-safe messaging
- Portfolio of real legal artifacts (10+ emails, 6 memos/letters, 3 agreements/excerpts)

## Structure & time commitment
- 13 weeks × 7 days (≈90 days), 45–60 minutes/day
- Weekly themes tied to real legal scenarios; daily micro-tasks + deliverables
- Milestones at Day 30, Day 60, Day 90 with rubrics and feedback loops

## How to use
- Create an Artifacts/ folder; save every deliverable by week/day
- Use Templates-&-Checklists.md to scaffold outputs
- Track progress in Tracking-Workbook.md (5-point rubric)
- Tools: Word/Google Docs (track changes), Grammarly/LanguageTool, a citation guide (Bluebook/ALWD), note system
