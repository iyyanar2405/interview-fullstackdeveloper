# 13 — Capstone & Teaching Portfolio (Week 13)

Theme: Curate, reflect, and present.

Daily plan
- Day 1: Select 6–10 artifacts (syllabus, briefs, slides, letters)
- Day 2: Portfolio structure (overview, samples, reflections)
- Day 3: Reflective statement (150–300 words) per artifact
- Day 4: Teaching philosophy (1–2 pages)
- Day 5: Include peer/student feedback summaries
- Day 6: Update CV and short bio; prepare a 5-min talk
- Day 7: Review

Deliverables
- Portfolio TOC; 2-page teaching philosophy; 6–10 curated artifacts
